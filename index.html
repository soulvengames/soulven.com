ABOUT US

Founded in 2016 as blackHound Studios and reimagined in 2023 as Soulven Games, we are an indie game studio based in Frankfurt am Main, Germany with a vision to redefine the landscape of 3D and game development. At Soulven Games, our mission is to become not just a leader but the best employer in the realm of 3D game development. We firmly believe that by fostering an outstanding work environment, we can create exceptional products and deliver unparalleled service. The essence of our name, Soulven, intertwines two powerful concepts: 'Soul' and 'Ven', derived from 'invention'. This fusion represents our commitment to nurturing interpersonal relationships while continuously driving innovation. Our approach is holistic; while we value the economic aspects of our business, we seamlessly integrate them into our broader processes, ensuring a balance between profitability and creativity.

THE TEAM

We're more than just an indie game development studio; we're a hub of imagination and technological prowess. Our core team, consisting of three dedicated and passionate individuals, brings a diverse array of skills and perspectives to every project. But that's not all – our creativity is amplified by our extensive network of talented freelancers. We pride ourselves on collaborating with a wide network of professionals who specialize in audio design, 3D modelling, and animation.
